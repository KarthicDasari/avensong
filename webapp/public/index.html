Option 1: Email
Scan, assemble and email the required pages in a SINGLE email to amenities@avensong.org.
✓ Please ensure PDF scans are legible – illegible files will not be processed.
✓ If emailing pictures, keep image file sizes around 1M each so they can be read for review and processing (illegible image files will be rejected).
✓ Please send all pages/images in ONE email – nobody can sort through emails to find and assemble your access packet for you.
✓ DO NOT SEND A LINK TO YOUR DOCUMENT. Avensong does not accept/access external links.

Option 2: mail (USPS)
If you cannot fax or email your packet, you can mail it to Avensong Community c/o Homeside Properties, Inc., 900 North Point Pkwy, Suite 325, Alpharetta, GA 30005.
DO NOT BRING PACKETS TO HPI OFFICES - HPI DOES NOT ACCEPT IN-PERSON DROP-OFFS.
